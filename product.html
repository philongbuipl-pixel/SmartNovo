Technology

A complete store, and the AI that runs it.

Two things ship together: a fully-fitted 21 m² retail unit, and an AI that does everything a staffed store needs a team for. It sells, watches, orders and promotes, and when it needs hands, it calls one human assistant.

The unit

Fully-fitted. Movable. Permit-light.

Legally classed as a vending machine, the 21 m² unit drops onto a site and trades in under an hour. No lease, no build-out, no six-month consent process. All it needs is land.

  1. App & QR entry

    The AI unlocks the door for the app or a QR code.

  2. Automatic doors

    Secure, sensor-controlled, contactless.

  3. Scan-and-pay checkout

    Self-checkout POS with card & BLIK. The AI reconciles every sale.

  4. Relocatable, stock and all

    The whole store, shelves still full, loads onto a small truck and reopens somewhere better in under an hour.

The AI brain

One AI is the manager,
cashier, guard and
marketing team.

It opens the doors, rings up every basket, watches the floor, adjusts prices, targets promotions to the customers in front of it and reorders stock before shelves run empty. When something needs hands, it dispatches one human assistant with an exact list.

AI-powered vision

Reads movement, never faces.

138-point skeletal tracking and behaviour recognition, built in-house over four years and running on commodity cameras at the edge.

Live · theft detection

Theft detection

The model recognises the gesture of concealment, a product going into a pocket or bag, and in the same second the customer gets a message that an unpaid item was detected and should be returned, while the operator is alerted.

  • Trained on theft gestures, not identities
  • Real-time operator + deterrent alerts
  • Reduces shrinkage where nothing watches the floor today
Live · fall detection

Fall detection

The same skeletal model recognises a collapse and pushes an immediate medical alert. That is safety cover in an unmanned store, and it is critical for hospital, campus and late-night sites.

  • Person down, recognised from body pose alone
  • Instant medical alert to the operator
  • No facial identification at any point

The platform

The AI manages the business. One dashboard shows you how.
