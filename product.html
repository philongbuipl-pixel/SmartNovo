Product & technology

A complete store, and the AI that runs it.

Two things ship together: a fully-fitted 21 m² retail unit, and four years of in-house computer vision that handles everything a staffed store needs a team for.

The unit

Fully-fitted. Movable. Permit-light.

Legally classed as a vending machine, the 21 m² unit drops onto a site and trades in under an hour, with no lease, no build-out and no six-month consent process.

  1. App & QR entry

    Unlock the door with the app or a QR code.

  2. Automatic doors

    Secure, sensor-controlled, contactless.

  3. Scan-and-pay checkout

    Self-checkout POS with card & BLIK.

  4. Relocatable

    Craned to a new site in under an hour as demand shifts.

Delivered, not built

From the back of a truck to open in an hour.

No contractors, no consent queue. The unit arrives fully-fitted, is craned into place, connects, and starts trading the same day, then relocates just as fast when you need it somewhere else.

The AI brain

One model runs every
system in the unit.

Entry, security, inventory and the whole fleet run on one computer-vision system, not a stack of disconnected sensors.

Computer vision

Reads movement, never faces.

138-point skeletal tracking and behaviour recognition, built in-house over four years and running on commodity cameras at the edge.

Live · theft detection

Theft detection

The model recognises the gesture of concealment, a product going into a pocket or bag, and in the same second the customer gets a message that an unpaid item was detected and should be returned, while the operator is alerted.

  • Trained on theft gestures, not identities
  • Real-time operator + deterrent alerts
  • Reduces shrinkage where nothing watches the floor today
Live · fall detection

Fall detection

The same skeletal model recognises a collapse and pushes an immediate medical alert. That is safety cover in an unmanned store, and it is critical for hospital, campus and late-night sites.

  • Person down, recognised from body pose alone
  • Instant medical alert to the operator
  • No facial identification at any point
